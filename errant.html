Nikhil Mathew & Varun Mathew

Errant-Labs + The Prolix Post, 2017

Skills Employed

Existing Outside Discipline
Making use of the learnings from the Visual Communications elective I took, I was able to begin consult in areas outside of architecture.
Foresight and Planning
I was able to juggle the work for my brother and my university assessments, and found the time and opportunity to consult with peers to get their input and feedback.
Creativity
I was able to evaluate the core ideas behind my clients vision and find methods for him to visually portray them.

This year I was approached by my brother to help redesign the user interface of his website and start creating artworks for his literary newsletter. This was an opportunity for me to start getting real experience with a public facing website and trying to come up with a clean, unique design aesthetic.

My user interface learnings and understandings that has shaped the design of his website include:

  • Colour palettes and colour design

  • Translating typographical concepts from print to web

  • Stylistic design decisions that reflect the underlying goal of his work

This was also an opportunity for me to start creating art and illustrations, something I've been wanting to do for a while now. I purchased a drawing tablet at the beginning of the year and this has been a great outlet to start experimenting and improving my drawing skills.
